Fire Foot Sanji (2018)

Photoshop
2550x3300 px

This is another poster that is inspired by an anime character named Sanji from the famous show One Piece. He is one of my favorite characters in the show so I wanted to make a piece that represented how I personally feel about the character. When creating this piece and many of my other posters, Ijust tried to make a visually appealing design with cool effects in Photoshop while keeping the different elements of art in mind. Poster design is something that I think I have a natural eye/gift for so I very much enjoy putting my skills into designs for the characters I love.
